History

Early history

United Lines Studio is a team composed by 4 members, selected on the base of their individual skills. The crew is an italian team, based in Italy and United Kingdom. Their past activities goes from computer technology to graphic and illustration for tabletop games and RPG's games, passing through disassembly video games for the study of the mechanics to realizing graphics on commissions for videogames of third parts. The team borns in 2016 and is now working on his first project together: an action rpg called Swords of Calengal.

After that

The team based the development of their first project on an home-made engine called XeEngine (Xeeynamo's Engine). Every tool, pixel assets and game elements, were created from scratch granting them an high freedom in creating features to insert in game. After winning some awards and due the growing interest of public, the Sound Design class of "International School of Comics of Padua" decided to take in charge the sound effects' creation of the game. The workflow undergoes a deadlines and milestones system which allows the team to set priorities to move forward faster.
